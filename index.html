About Us

The Lily Foundation for Patient Advocacy & Empowerment is a nonprofit committed to supporting families during medical crises and promoting preventative health education, especially in underserved communities across Alabama and surrounding states.

We honor the memory of Lillie R. Thomas through advocacy, compassion, and education — empowering patients to make informed healthcare decisions when it matters most.

Programs & Services

  • Real-time medical advocacy and support via phone, text, and email
  • Workshops on preventative care and patient rights
  • Support navigating end-of-life decisions and post-mortem guidance
  • Referral network to legal, social, and healthcare resources
